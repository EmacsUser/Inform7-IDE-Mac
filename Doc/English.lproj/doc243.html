17.4. Where extensions are kept

This depends on the operating system we use. On Mac OS X, extensions live in

~/Library/Inform/Extensions/

where "~" signifies the user's home folder. The extension "Following People by Mary Brown" would live in

~/Library/Inform/Extensions/Mary Brown/Following People

(no filename extension is used). A similar arrangement is used under Windows: extensions live in

My Documents\Inform\Extensions

and our example extension would be stored as:

My Documents\Inform\Extensions\Mary Brown\Following People

Again, no filename extension is used.
