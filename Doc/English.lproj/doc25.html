2.16. Possessions and clothing

We have seen how to place objects in rooms, and in containers or on supporters, but not how to make them begin in the player's possession, which we do as follows:

A brass lantern and an old brass key are initially carried.

Clothes are simply things which have the either/or property of being "wearable": if something has this property then the player will be allowed to wear it, provided it can first be picked up. It will then have the either/or property of being "worn". To start the player off wearing something, we need to specify both that the player has it and that the player wears it:

The cardinal's hat is initially carried. The hat is worn.
