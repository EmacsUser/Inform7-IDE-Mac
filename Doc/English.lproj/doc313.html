22.1. Emily Short - Singing bug part 2

Reported by: Emily Short / Date: 5 November 2005

Nature: The source looks legal and produces a playable game, but the game does not do what it should

Build: 3C61

Platform: Mac OS X

Code example:

The Academy is a room. Aristotle is a man in the Academy. Aristotle is carrying an electric razor. The razor is a device.

The player wears a himation. The player carries an eel from the Copaic lake. The eel is edible.

Instead of examining the himation for the first time:
    say "This is the first time you've looked at the himation."

Instead of examining the himation for the second time:
    say "This is the second time you've looked at the himation. I'm still not going to tell you anything about it. Try again later."

Instead of examining the himation for the third time:
    say "This is the third time you've looked at the himation. Good, you're a patient sort."

What goes wrong:

>x himation

You see nothing special about the himation.

>g

This is the first time you've looked at the himation.

>g

This is the second time you've looked at the himation. I'm still not going to tell you anything about it. Try again later.

>g

>g
