7.9. Repeats without numbers

One other form of "repeat" is also allowed (and others will certainly be added in later releases of Inform).

repeat with item running through contents of Marley Wood begin;
    ... phrases ...
end repeat;

Once again we create a new value, "item", of only temporary validity. This time the phrases are repeated once for each of the things in the given room (or it may be a thing such as a container, of course). If there's nothing in the given place, the phrases are never run through at all.
