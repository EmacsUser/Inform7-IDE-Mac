Chapter 10: Rules

10.1. On rules

We have seen that actions, the great sluice of change in the simulated world, work their way through a great mass of rules, each applying in a different circumstance. The chapter on actions vaguely described these rules as belonging to different "rulebooks": for instance, the "instead rules" or the "carry out dropping rules".

Inform is largely a rule-based system, and this extends beyond actions to other considerations, too. The "every turn" rules, for instance, form another rulebook but which has nothing to do with actions as such.

This chapter goes into how rules and rulebooks work in much more detail, and we shall also see how to create not just new rules but new rulebooks.
