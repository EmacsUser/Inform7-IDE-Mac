Run-time problem

Not all problems can be detected at compile time, that is, when the Go or Replay buttons are clicked. Some emerge only during play, and these are called "run-time problems".

Here a problem arose because you tried to move a backdrop to a specific place or item. Backdrops can, in principle, begin play in any combination of the rooms, and most backdrops never move at all - they are like a fixed painted backcloth in a stage set. If they do move, the restriction is that they can only move to single regions, and this restriction seems to have been broken here.
